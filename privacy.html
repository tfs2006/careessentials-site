Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website promoting Care Essentials products. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Our website does not collect personal information directly. However, we use analytics tools to collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • IP address (anonymized)

This information is collected solely for analytics purposes to help us understand how visitors use our site and improve user experience.

ClickBank Affiliate Relationship

This website is an affiliate marketing site. We participate in the ClickBank affiliate program and may earn commissions when you click on links to Care Essentials products and make purchases. When you click on affiliate links, you will be redirected to ClickBank's platform, which has its own privacy policy and data collection practices.

We do not receive any personal information about customers who purchase through our affiliate links. All transactions, payment processing, and customer data are handled by ClickBank and the product vendor.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website and third-party services may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Track affiliate referrals
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Remember user preferences

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality and affiliate tracking.

No User Accounts

Our website does not require user registration or account creation. We do not collect usernames, passwords, or personal profile information.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and site functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • ClickBank: Handles all product sales and transactions
  • Web Analytics Tools: May collect anonymized usage data

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal information directly, there is minimal risk of data breaches related to personal data on our site. However, we implement reasonable security measures to protect our website infrastructure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of cookies through your browser settings
  • Request information about data collected through analytics
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
